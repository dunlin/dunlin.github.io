What's this?

Imagine video conferencing just working, being simple & fun actually. That's Dunlin, a tool to communicate with others via video, just inside your desktop browser window. It requires no separate download and the communication is encrypted by default.

Dunlins focus is ease of use. Share a link with all the members of your conference. Now you're ready to talk to each other.
