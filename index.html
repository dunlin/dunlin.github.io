What's this?

Imagine video conferencing being simple, just working and fun actually. This is Dunlin. It's a tool to communicate with others via video just inside your browser window. Requires no separate download and the communication is encrypted by default.

Dunlins focus is ease of use. Share a link with all the members of your conference and you're ready to talk to each other.
